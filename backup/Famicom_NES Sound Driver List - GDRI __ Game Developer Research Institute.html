Famicom/NES Sound Driver List

From Game Developer Research Institute
Jump to: navigation, search

The Famicom/NES Sound Driver List is meant to be a list of all video games released on the NES, Famicom, and the Famicom Disk System. This includes unlicensed/pirated and homebrew games. The point of this list is to determine either the developer of a game that went uncredited, or to know whether or not a company outsourced sound and if so, what/who the company was.

Games with a (?) represent unconfirmed sound drivers. (FDS) represents games which contain the Famicom Disk System expansion sound channel (though not necessarily used). Red text also represents unconfirmed drivers.
